Cookie Policy

Date: 12-3-25

This Cookie Policy explains how SmileCare Dental Clinic uses cookies and similar technologies to recognize you when you visit our website at www.smilecare.com.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small data files that are placed on your device when you visit a website. They help us understand user behavior and improve your browsing experience.

How We Use Cookies

  • To remember user preferences
  • To analyze website traffic and user interactions
  • To enable social media sharing and functionality

Your Choices

You can choose to accept or decline cookies via the banner shown on your first visit or through your browser settings. However, disabling cookies may affect your experience.

Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for the site to function properly.
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how visitors interact with the website.
  • Functionality Cookies: Enhance the user experience by remembering choices.
